Consumption Per Capita Forecast Condensation

Once we identified the best model for each country and database, that model was used to predict the data 13 years ahead.

All the prediction models for the per capita consumption are calculated with the Random Forest method. For these top five European countries, the model shows irregular trends with high and lows over the forecasting period that, in overall terms, end with almost the same current consumption level, except for Sweden, who is the only country with a modest positive trend at the end of the period
